Before adding an image, record the local filename, original source, permitted use, attribution terms, background variants, and review date here. Do not download a logo from an unrelated search result or hotlink an advertiser asset.

The text-badge fallback is designed for light card backgrounds, uses a fixed square size, wraps no text inside the mark, and remains stable at mobile widths. If a future image fails, the card should return to this badge rather than collapse its media area.
