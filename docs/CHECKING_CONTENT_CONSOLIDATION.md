# Checking Content Consolidation

## Primary national pillar

`/best-banks-for-checking` is the canonical national pillar for broad queries such as “best bank for checking” and “best banks for checking accounts.” It covers national, online, regional, and credit-union comparison factors without claiming one universal winner.

## Supporting pages

- `/best-bank-for-checking` remains live for existing links but canonicals to the plural pillar.
- `/best-checking-accounts-florida` keeps its distinct Florida intent.
- `/checking-account-bonuses` focuses on promotion mechanics.
- `/bank-bonuses` remains the broader checking-and-savings offer hub.

No redirect is applied in this release. Internal links should prefer `/best-banks-for-checking`; Search Console behavior can guide a future redirect decision.
